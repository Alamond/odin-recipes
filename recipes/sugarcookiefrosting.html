Sugar cookie frosting

Description

This sugar cookie frosting recipe is super quick and easy to make. Add any food coloring you'd like to decorate your favorite sugar cookies.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • Â½ cup shortening
  • 5 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • food coloring (Optional)

Steps

  1. Beat confectioners' sugar and shortening together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth. Gradually beat in milk and vanilla until stiff and glossy, about 5 minutes. Color with food coloring if desired.
  2. Enjoy!
